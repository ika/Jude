# Privacy Policy

This application respects your privacy and is designed with simplicity and security
in mind. No personal data is collected, stored, or processed at any time. You are not required to
create an account or log in to use the app, ensuring complete anonymity and ease of access. The app
functions entirely on your device, without transmitting information to external servers.
Furthermore, no data is shared, sold, or disclosed to any third parties. By using this application,
you can be assured of a private, secure, and transparent experience.
